Lab 10: Time of Flight Imaging Application with Adabox Car.

In this final lab, we are working with a robotic car attached with an 8x8 Lidar sensor to detect the front obstacle of the car. Whenever we encounter some obstacles infront the car, we should have the car perform some actions to avoid the collision, such as back off, left, right turn, or others depend on the situation.
